Environect

Our Software Service provides an e-Platform for Three different major parties involved in Waste Management Industry who are
Waste generators, Recyclers and Consumers.


- We are connecting Waste generators with recyclers with a digital platform where recyclers can obtain their waste from waste generators.
- And we are connecting the recyclers with waste generators and we provide a platform where the recyclers can sell their products.
- We are providing a platform for the consumers where they can buy recycled products from the recyclers directly.
